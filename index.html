📧 How to Enable Real Emails (Free — takes 5 minutes)

This website uses EmailJS to send real emails — completely free, no server needed.

Step 1: Go to emailjs.com → Create free account
Step 2: Add Email Service → connect your Gmail (cleanbyyami@gmail.com)
Step 3: Create two Email Templates (instructions below)
Step 4: Paste your keys below and click Save

Template Setup Guide

In EmailJS, create Template 1: "Booking Received" — sent to customer when they book.
Use these variables in your template:
{{customer_name}} — customer's name
{{customer_email}} — their email
{{service_type}} — type of cleaning
{{home_type}} — home type
{{booking_date}} — preferred date
{{price_estimate}} — price range
{{business_phone}} — your phone

Create Template 2: "Booking Confirmed" — sent when you click Confirm in admin.
Same variables + {{confirmed_message}} for any note you add.
